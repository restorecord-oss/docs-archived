## Missing Permissions
If you're getting error messages such as:
- Missing permissions

You should look at the possible solutions:

   | **Possible Solution** 
| :---: |
| Is your bot in the Server?  | 
| Does your bot have permissions? |
| Make sure that the `bot` role is above the `verified` role.  |
